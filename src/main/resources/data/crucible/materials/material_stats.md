# Material Stats Meaning

## General
**Durability:** Durability * k (some number?) * p (peice durability: e.g. higher for chestplate than boots) of item  
**Heat Resistance:** Heat required to smelt  
**Enchantability:** How good of enchants you get and how cheap/expensive it is to apply enchants in anvils. At low levels, can't have high-level enchants and at high can get higher than normal level enchants in an enchating table  

## Armour
**Weight:** Increased hunger usage if in the mid-range and decreased movement speed if very high  
**Strength:** Armour level  
**Hardness:** Armour Toughness  
**Heat Resistance:** If very high some innate fire protection

## Tools
**Weight:** (Mining) Speed  
**Strength:** Mining level (like wood's mining level would be 1, fist 0, iron 3, etc.)
